Get fit the FUN way at Slammers Beach Volleyball club!

Slammers Beach Volleyball club offers FUN and FITNESS for the whole family!

Volleyball is a great sport that is safe, fun and suitable for people of all ages. As it does not require extreme fitness, volleyball is the perfect low-impact sport for people who want to get fit without pushing them to their limit. For the more competitive ones who like a challenge, the sport is fantastic because games can be really exciting and intense, but still safe as there is always a net between you and your opponents.

Volleyball at a glance...

  • Safe for all levels of fitness**
  • Low-impact workout
  • Suitable for men and women
  • For all ages
  • FUN for the whole family!

Whether you are a regular player or looking to try a new sport, everyone is welcome at Slammers =)

After the game, chill out with friends or meet new ones at our licensed bar in the evenings... Happy Hour from 6-7pm daily!
