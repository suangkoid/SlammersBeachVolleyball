Goals

The Slammers Beach Volleyball website aims to increase revenue for the company by increasing the number of customers, primarily from playing volleyball and participating in the Slammers tournament, but also from other activities such as socialising at the licensed bar and venue hire for functions.

The target outcome is an increase of at least 50% in the number of customers for the company after the website is launched.

Success Evaluation

The site's success will be evaluated by measuring any increase in sales after the website has been launched within a 3-month period, and comparing that figure to the 3 months prior to the site's launch. All other advertising media will remain consistent during that period.

Target Audience

1. Local residents from the youth age group (15-30 years old) are who the site is aimed at.

2. The site will have a simple yet sporty design with vibrant colours and images, and text which is easy-to-read. The site is designed to work across all devices and browsers on desktops, laptops, mobile phones or tablets.
