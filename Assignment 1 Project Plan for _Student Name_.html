Goals

The Slammers Beach Volleyball website aims to increase revenue for the company by promoting the games and activites available to attract new customers..The target outcome is an increase of at least 50% in the number of customers for the company after the website is launched.

Success Evaluation

The site's success will be evaluated by measuring any increase in sales after the website has been launched within a 3-month period, and comparing that figure to the 3 months prior to the site's launch. All other advertising media will remain consistent during that period.

Target Audience

1. The site is aimed at local residents from the youth age group (15-30 years old).

2. The site will have a simple layout with vibrant colours and the use of appropriate images. Text will be easy-to-read, with highlighted text in parts for quick scanning of key information. Call-to-action buttons will be used to prompt user reaction to find out more by contacting the company or by registering online to play.
